Edutrip Summary Jogja - Solo

In the 8th grade of Santa Laurensia middle school they hold an educational trip with 3 locations in mind that students are able to choose which are, Taiwan, Singapore, and Yogyakarta. However, due to the massive demand of many students who want to go to Taiwan, Singapore and Yogyakarta always barely had enough students to operate. So to save costs, as of this year, Singapore will be unavailable and Yogyakarta will be extended to include Solo.

Locations in that we visit in Yogyakarta are for the first 4 days, village Pentingsari, Museum Ullen Sentalu, Museum Gunung Api Merapi, Candi Prambanan, Sendratari Ramayana, Keraton Yogyakarta, Pusat Pelatihan Batik Winotosastro, Gereja HKY Ganjuran, Malioboro, Institut Seni Indonesia, Rumah Atsiri Indonesia, Museum Manusa Purba Sangiran Krikilan, and Museum De Tjolomadoe.
